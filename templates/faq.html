Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TrafficSafe

What is TrafficSafe?

TrafficSafe is a comprehensive traffic accident reporting and management system for Tunisia. It helps citizens report accidents, authorities analyze traffic patterns, and promotes road safety through data-driven insights and public intelligence.

How do I report an accident?

You can report an accident by logging into your account and clicking "Report Accident" from the navigation menu. Fill in the location, date, time, severity level, and any additional details. Your report will be reviewed by authorities and help improve road safety.

Is my accident report anonymous?

While you need to create an account to submit reports, your personal information is protected and kept confidential. Government authorities only access necessary details for investigation and safety improvement purposes.

What data does the dashboard show?

The dashboard displays comprehensive traffic accident statistics including total accidents, injury and fatality counts, accident severity levels, distribution by location and cause, temporal trends, and weather-related factors. This data helps identify high-risk areas and times for targeted safety interventions.

How can I access safety resources?

Visit the Services page to access emergency contacts, traffic speed limits, fuel prices, fine calculators, route planners, and driving safety tips. These resources are available to all users and help promote responsible driving habits.

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started" on the landing page or "Sign Up" in the authentication area. Provide your email, full name, date of birth, and create a secure password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Is the platform available on mobile?

Yes, TrafficSafe is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. You can access all features from any device through your web browser.

How often is traffic data updated?

Traffic accident data and statistics are updated in real-time as reports are submitted. Traffic news and alerts refresh automatically to provide the latest information about road conditions and safety concerns.

What does the dark mode do?

Dark mode switches the interface to a darker color scheme that's easier on the eyes during low-light conditions. You can toggle between light and dark modes using the theme switcher in the account menu.

How do I report a problem or give feedback?

Use the Contact Us page to send us feedback, report issues, or share suggestions. We value your input and use it to continuously improve TrafficSafe. You can also reach us at trafficaccidentstn@gmail.com.
