Stay Safe

Essential Safety Tips

Simple actions that can save your life and the lives of others on the road.

01

No Phone Zone

Put your phone away while driving. No text is worth your life.

02

Respect Speed Limits

Speed limits exist for a reason. Slow down and arrive alive.

03

Always Wear Seatbelts

Buckle up every trip, every time. It takes 3 seconds.

04

Never Drink & Drive

If you drink, don't drive. Arrange alternative transportation.

05

Stay Alert

Keep your eyes on the road and mind on driving at all times.

06

Maintain Your Vehicle

Regular maintenance prevents mechanical failures on the road.
